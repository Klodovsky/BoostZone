BoostZone

- Crowdfunding Platform | Developed with PHP Codeigniter

  • Created on: 25/Jul/2019
  • Developed by: Khaled BEN HASSEN


  • There is more happiness in giving more than recieving ,BoostZone is developed for creators to find and build meaningful relationships with their community and designed to help digital content creators build relationships and provide their best content to their loving supporters. With this platform, creators can now just focus on creating quality content and build momentum around their community.

    In this Platform, people register to support their creators for their content. In return, creators open up their creative practice to their contributors by sharing their processes, learning from their fields and many other rewards. It is a platform for creators to build a community of dedicated and meaningful supporters around the work they create.

Product Configuration

Step One

Git clone files from Github and you will see the following folders :

/BoostZone
/Documetation
/License
 And upload the all files from "BoostZone" folder into the Live/Local server folder

Step Two

Create a New Database and import the boostzone-db.sql file in the phpmyadmin.

File Path: BoostZone/Database/boostzone-db.sql

Step Three

Update the following Configurations
Database
File Path: application/config/database.php
'hostname' => 'localhost',
'username' => 'xxxxxxxxxx',
'password' => 'xxxxxxxxxxx',
'database' => 'xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx',

Google Login API
File Path: application/config/google.php
Change Apikey
Change client_id
Change client_secret

Razor Payment Gateway API
application/views/home.php
Line no : 290
application/views/videos.php
Line no : 158
PS: This is a demo version for real payments you will need to setup your own Razorpay account .


Step Four

Use the below login credentials to access the Admin Panel,
URL: https://www.example.com/login
Username: admin@admin.com
Password: admin123

Details of the Product

Home Page

Home is provide the details notes of how the platform works

New Creators Post will be showcased here and Donator can donate amount and follow the favorite creator

About us

A standard About page that can be customized according to your needs.

Contact Page

Lets users know the company address and contact details of yours. Working contact form is available for collect the user/inquries details

Admin Dashboard

Here you see the overview of the platform users and total amount donated. And you can Find the last five Creators and Donators

Admin Creator View

Here we list out the all creaors in details and Once click the Register ID/Name you will see the Profile view of the creator in details like video post, donator details, and more core features implemented.
Export Options available for offline purpose.
Admin can only transfer their donation amount by manually in the Version 1.0.

Admin Donator View

Donator View gives the details how many people are available in the platform and their donation details. And more core functionalities availble

Admin Setting

Here you can change the profile details, Website Name, logo, SEO details, and payment gateway details.


Login & Registeration

Creator and Donator can sign-up/sign-in using their Email ID, Password or Google platform.
Once Sign-up, platform redirect to choose "Who they are?" i.e. Creator or Donator.
If they are a Donator, they will see the "Dashboard" and "Creator categories Page" to see their favorite creator/new one videos.
If they are a Creator, they will redirected to "Choose a category Page" after we take to the Dashboard Page



Donator Page

Donator can login and see the details of their profile, Last five Donation details and Folloing details



Creator Page

Creator can Post their Videos to raise fund or upcoming famous video of their creation.
Videos start showing once they enter the details of CHannel Name, started year, First Name, Last Name, and Bank Details.
Creator's can also choose the categories of the videos. Categories are available in "My Category Page".
